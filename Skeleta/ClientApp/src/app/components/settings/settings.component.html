What if clr-tabs are inside a flexbox-ed parent element..
Currently there's no way to know which clr-tab is active unless we see if its content is not visible. 
  
  <clr-tab>
    <clr-tab-content[aria-hidden="false"] ...

vscIfTabActive to the rescue.. if-tab-active.directive.ts 

  
  <clr-tab vscIfTabActive [activeClass]="'activeTab'"
    <clr-tab-content[aria-hidden="false"] ...
